Keeping up to date

You must keep your knowledge and skills up to date throughout your career, actively seeking out development opportunities that will meet your learning needs and priorities. You should:

  1. Routinely review and update knowledge of legal, practice, policy, regulatory and procedural framework.
  2. Keep up to date with relevant literature and research.
  3. Listen and learn from others including other professionals, individuals, relatives and carers.
  4. Seek assistance with critical gaps in your own knowledge and skills.
  5. Use your learning to support the improvement of services.
