Leadership and management skills

You should make particular efforts to develop your knowledge and skills as a manager and leader of excellent social care practice. This could include a range of activities: seeking feedback on your style and practice, reading, attending courses, visits and placements, contributing to professional groups and forums, accessing or contributing to research.
